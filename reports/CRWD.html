Investment Thesis

HOLD

CrowdStrike demonstrates elite SaaS unit economics with 74% gross margins and exceptional $915M in free cash flow (26% margin) despite near-term profitability headwinds. The July 2024 global outage significantly impacted operations, leading to temporary margin pressure and increased S&M spend to rebuild customer trust.

With a Rule of 40 score of 47.2% (21% growth + 26% FCF margin), CRWD remains fundamentally strong despite the -1.0% net margin. The company maintains its market-leading position in cloud-native endpoint security with robust customer retention and strong cash generation capability.

Recovery Timeline: Management is focused on operational excellence and customer satisfaction. Watch for improved operating leverage in FY2027 as one-time costs subside and S&M efficiency normalizes.
