Investment Thesis

STRONG BUY

Amazon has reached a major profitability inflection point in FY2024, with operating margin expanding an exceptional 4.3 percentage points to 10.7%. The company generated $59.2B in net income (+94.6% YoY) while maintaining strong 11% revenue growth to $638B. This is a rare combination of scale, growth, and margin expansion.

All three critical margins improved substantially: Gross +1.9pp, Operating +4.3pp, Net +4.0pp. AWS continues to drive high-margin revenue, while the retail business has achieved new levels of efficiency through automation and logistics optimization. FCF of $40.8B (+11.5% YoY) despite massive $75B CapEx investment demonstrates exceptional cash generation.

Key Strengths: (1) AWS dominance in cloud infrastructure with 30%+ margins, (2) Retail operating leverage finally materializing at scale, (3) Advertising business growing rapidly with minimal incremental costs, (4) Prime ecosystem creating powerful moat. Key Risk: Elevated CapEx ($75B, +55%) pressures FCF, but this is strategic investment in AWS capacity to capture AI workload growth.
