The valuation is justified by: (1) Dominant market position in e-commerce and cloud computing, (2) AWS profit engine generating 30%+ operating margins and majority of operating income, (3) Massive scale advantages creating powerful network effects and cost benefits, and (4) Diversified revenue streams including advertising, subscriptions, and third-party seller services.

The company's competitive moat is exceptionally wide, with unmatched logistics infrastructure and cloud capabilities. Current valuation offers reasonable entry point for quality-focused investors.
