Investment Thesis

HOLD / WAIT

Cboe Global Markets is a dominant exchange operator with excellent profitability (26.8% operating margin, 18.6% net margin) and strong cash generation ($1,039.7M FCF). The company demonstrated solid revenue growth of 8.5% YoY, driven by options volume growth and market share gains.

Primary Concern: All margins are declining YoY despite revenue growth. Gross margin down 0.2pp, operating margin down 1.2pp, and net margin down 1.5pp. This suggests rising costs are outpacing revenue growth, potentially due to competitive pressures or technology investments ($60.9M CapEx, +35.3% YoY).

Valuation Risk: Trading at P/E of 31.2x (vs sector average ~18x) and PEG of 3.67x suggests significant overvaluation. At current price, the risk/reward is unfavorable given margin compression.

Positives: FCF remains strong at 25.4% margin, consistent profitability, and dominant market position in options and volatility products. EPS growth of 1.1% with share buybacks (105.5M diluted shares vs 106.2M prior year).
