Investment Thesis

HOLD / ACCUMULATE

Microsoft remains a world-class compounder with exceptional profitability (41.8% operating margin, 34.1% net margin) and robust cash generation ($59.5B FCF). The company is in the midst of a massive infrastructure build-out to support Azure and AI workloads, with CapEx increasing 17.7% YoY to $28.1B.

Revenue growth of 6.9% reflects Microsoft's maturity but understates the strength in Intelligent Cloud (+21% YoY) and AI adoption across the product portfolio. Net income was flat YoY (-0.5%) primarily due to higher tax expenses ($51.3B vs $17.0B), not operational weakness. Operating income grew 6.2%, demonstrating core business strength.

Key Risk: Declining FCF (-8.7% YoY) due to elevated CapEx spending. However, this is a strategic investment in competitive positioning for the AI era. FCF margin of 28.1% remains excellent and should expand as infrastructure investments mature.
