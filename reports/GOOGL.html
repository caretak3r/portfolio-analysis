Investment Thesis

STRONG BUY

Alphabet demonstrates world-class financial performance with operating margins exceeding 32% and net margins of 34%. The company's Q3 2025 net income of $35B represents a 33% YoY increase, showcasing exceptional operational leverage as AI-powered products drive engagement and monetization.

While gross margins at 59.6% are slightly below the 60% threshold for an A grade in tech, the company's aggressive $63.6B capex investment in AI infrastructure positions it as a leader in the AI revolution. The 14% revenue growth combined with 33% net income growth demonstrates improving operational efficiency and pricing power in advertising and cloud computing.

Strong FCF generation of $48.7B provides ample resources for innovation, buybacks, and maintaining competitive moats in Search, YouTube, and Google Cloud. The company's ability to generate $112B in operating cash flow while investing heavily in future growth makes it an exceptional long-term compounder.
