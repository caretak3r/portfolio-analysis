Investment Thesis

HOLD

Leidos Holdings demonstrates steady operational improvement with margins expanding across the board. The company achieved 5.5% revenue growth to $13.0B (9M) while improving operating margins from 11.2% to 12.6%, showcasing improving efficiency in government contract execution.

Most encouraging is the 19.3% surge in operating income to $1.64B and 13.7% net income growth to $1.12B. Gross margins improved by 100 basis points to 18.2%, reflecting better project mix and operational execution. The company generates $1.26B in operating cash flow (9.7% margin), though free cash flow declined to $0.88B due to increased investing activities.

As a defense and government IT services provider, Leidos operates in a stable, mission-critical sector with long-term contracts. The company's focus on digital modernization, cybersecurity, and national security solutions positions it well for sustained demand. However, growth is constrained by the nature of government contracting, and margins are structurally lower than pure software companies.

Key Catalyst Government IT modernization
