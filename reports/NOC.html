Investment Thesis

HOLD

Northrop Grumman maintains stable profitability with 10.7% operating margins and 9.1% net margins, typical for a mature defense contractor. However, revenue declined 0.3% YoY and all major profitability metrics showed modest deterioration.

The most concerning trend is the 91.6% decline in free cash flow to just $72M (0.2% margin) from $859M in the prior year period. This appears to be driven by working capital timing rather than structural issues, but warrants close monitoring. Operating cash flow fell 52.5% to $860M.

As a key defense prime contractor for the U.S. military with exposure to strategic programs (B-21 Raider, GBSD, space systems), NOC benefits from a strong backlog and long-term visibility. The company maintains solid gross margins (13%) and lean G&A (2.3% of revenue).
