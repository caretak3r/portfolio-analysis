Investment Thesis

HOLD

Costco remains a best-in-class retail operator with an impenetrable moat driven by its membership model, scale advantages, and operational excellence. Q1 FY2025 demonstrates consistent execution with 8.3% revenue growth, 11.3% net income growth, and industry-leading 3.7% operating margins.

However, at a P/E of 55.7x (vs. 15-20x retail average) and PEG of 6.7x, the stock is trading at a significant premium. While Costco deserves a quality premium given its membership moat and consistent performance, current valuation leaves limited upside. Fair value estimated at $750-$850, suggesting 12-27% downside risk.

Recommendation: HOLD for existing positions, WAIT for pullback below $850 to accumulate. This is a "forever hold" quality business, but valuation discipline matters.
