Investment Thesis

HOLD / QUALIFIED BUY

ASML is the undisputed leader in semiconductor lithography with an unassailable competitive moat. The company's monopoly position in EUV technology makes it an essential enabler of the AI revolution and next-generation chip manufacturing. Financial performance is exceptional across all metrics (A+ grades in margins and cash flow), with 29.4% net margins and a Rule of 40 score of 53.9.

However, current valuation at 44.8x P/E and trading above analyst consensus suggests limited near-term upside. The company faces geopolitical risks with ~29% revenue exposure to China and regulatory headwinds from export restrictions.

Key Catalysts: AI infrastructure boom, TSMC expansion ($30B+ annual capex), Intel turnaround, and next-gen High-NA EUV systems ($380M per unit) entering production. Main Risks: Valuation compression, China exposure, cyclical industry dynamics, and customer concentration (80% from top 3 customers).
