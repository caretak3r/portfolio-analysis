Investment Thesis

HOLD

Synopsys remains a dominant player in EDA and semiconductor IP, delivering 15% revenue growth with excellent gross margins (77%). However, all three key margins declined significantly YoY: gross margin down 2.7pp, operating margin down 9.1pp, and net margin down 18pp.

The compression appears to be driven by elevated R&D spending (35% of revenue), likely due to acquisition integration costs and AI-driven EDA platform investments. Free cash flow remains strong at 19.1% margin ($1.35B), demonstrating underlying business health.

Key Concerns: 1) Margin compression must reverse in FY26, 2) High R&D spend needs to translate to product leadership, 3) Operating leverage should improve as integration costs normalize. Q4 showed strong recovery with 19.9% net margin, suggesting trend may be stabilizing.
