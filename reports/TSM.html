Investment Thesis

HOLD / ACCUMULATE ON DIPS

TSM is the undisputed leader in semiconductor foundry services with an irreplaceable position in the global technology supply chain. The company commands ~60% market share and benefits from structural tailwinds including AI chip demand, data center expansion, and advanced node migration. With 56% gross margins and 40% net margins on a hardware business, TSM demonstrates exceptional operational excellence and pricing power.

Revenue growth of 33.9% YoY is extraordinary for a company of TSM's scale ($91.9B revenue). The company is the critical supplier for NVIDIA, Apple, and AMD's advanced AI chips. Technology leadership in 3nm mass production with 2nm coming in 2025 ensures continued pricing power and market dominance. Operating margin expanded 3.1pp to 45.7%, demonstrating operating leverage as advanced nodes scale.

Key Risk: Geopolitical tensions with China represent the primary concern, though US fab expansion in Arizona mitigates this somewhat. Valuation is premium (P/E ~45.5x) but justified by unmatched competitive position. Competition from Intel and Samsung in foundry remains distant. Customer concentration (Apple, NVIDIA) is notable but these relationships are deeply entrenched.

✅ Key Catalysts

  • • AI Boom: Critical supplier for NVIDIA, AMD AI chips
  • • Technology Leadership: 3nm mass production, 2nm coming 2025
  • • US Expansion: Arizona fabs reduce geopolitical risk
  • • Pricing Power: Can pass costs to customers
  • • Margin Expansion: Advanced nodes command premium pricing

⚠️ Key Risks

  • • Geopolitical: Taiwan tensions with China
  • • Valuation: Trading at premium multiples (P/E ~45x)
  • • Customer Concentration: Reliance on Apple, NVIDIA
  • • Cyclical Industry: Semiconductor downturns possible
  • • Competition: Intel, Samsung investing in foundry
