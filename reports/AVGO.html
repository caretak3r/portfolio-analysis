Investment Thesis

STRONG BUY

Broadcom demonstrates exceptional financial performance with a Rule of 40 score of 66% - significantly above the 40% threshold for elite businesses. The company achieved 23.9% revenue growth to $63.9B while simultaneously expanding operating margins from 26% to 40%, showcasing remarkable operating leverage.

Most impressive is the 292% surge in net income to $23.1B, driven by improved gross margins (67.8%), operational efficiency (SG&A down to 6.6% of revenue), and a favorable tax position. The company generates $26.9B in free cash flow (42.1% margin), providing substantial capital for shareholder returns and strategic investments.

Broadcom's diversified portfolio across semiconductors (networking, broadband, wireless) and infrastructure software (VMware, Symantec) positions it as a critical enabler of AI infrastructure, cloud computing, and 5G connectivity. The company's pricing power, operational scale, and mission-critical products create a formidable moat.
