Investment Thesis

HOLD / WAIT FOR DIP

Constellation Software is a world-class compounding machine with a proven vertical market software (VMS) acquisition strategy. The company posted strong FY24 results: 19.7% revenue growth, 29.3% EPS growth, and exceptional 21.1% FCF margin.

The Paradox: While the business fundamentals are excellent, the margins tell a different story than typical SaaS companies. Gross margin of 35.9% (vs 70%+ for pure SaaS) reflects Constellation's unique model: mission-critical niche software with significant implementation services and on-premise components. This isn't a weakness—it's the moat. High switching costs and sticky customer relationships compensate for lower headline margins.

Key Strengths: (1) Capital allocation mastery—$1.5B deployed in acquisitions, (2) Free cash flow generation is stellar at $2.1B (21% margin), (3) Operating leverage improving with 24% operating income growth outpacing revenue growth, (4) Decentralized management model scales well.

Valuation Concern: At 65x P/E and 3.3 PEG ratio, the stock trades at a significant premium. While quality deserves a premium, current pricing leaves little room for error. A 20-30% pullback would present a compelling entry point for long-term compounding.
