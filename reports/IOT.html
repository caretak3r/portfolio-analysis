Investment Thesis

BUY

Samsara has reached a critical inflection point with Q3 FY26 marking its first profitable quarter ($7.8M net income). The company demonstrates elite SaaS unit economics with 77% gross margins and is rapidly approaching sustained profitability with operating margins improving 13.8pp YoY.

With 30% revenue growth and 12.4% FCF margin, Samsara achieves a Rule of 40 score of 42.4% — a hallmark of exceptional SaaS businesses. The land-and-expand model is validated by 2,990 customers with >$100K ARR (up 30% YoY).
