Investment Thesis

HOLD

Teradyne is navigating a cyclical downturn in semiconductor testing demand with revenue growth of only 1.9% YoY. However, the company maintains exceptional gross margins of 58.7% and generates strong free cash flow of $302M (14.4% margin).

The business demonstrates operational excellence with solid B-grade fundamentals, though the Rule of 40 score of 16.3 reflects the current industry headwinds. Operating margins compressed to 16.9% (from 21.3%) as the company navigates the downcycle while maintaining R&D investment at 17.1% of revenue.

Cyclical Entry Point: Teradyne's strong balance sheet ($273M cash) and history of 20%+ operating margins during upcycles makes this a potential accumulation zone for patient investors. The company returned capital through $185M in share repurchases (9M).
